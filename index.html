About Us

URANUS is a micro – small sized Engineering Solutions Company, operating in the Construction and Mining Sector. The advocacy and promotion of economic independence in youth gave birth to an idea behind the formation of URANUS SOLUTIONS (PTY) LTD.

As a 100% black female owned and managed company, URANUS is committed to the principles of Broad Based Black Economic Empowerment and strives to address the challenges of youth unemployment and skills developments.

Vision

URANUS pioneers towards contributing to transformation, service delivery and sustainable growth through our commitment to provide quality services in an environment where our clients come first. We seek to be supplier and contractor of preference and further develop our country through the provision of employment and training opportunities.

MISSION

We commit to providing unparalleled service and quality workmanship with transparent pricing. Our mission is to set the benchmark by which all other contractors are measured. URANUS works with the intent of keeping our customers for a lifetime by exceeding their expectations and providing competitive and cost effective solutions.

Passion

We show a vast amount of pride, enthusiasm and dedication in what we do. We are committed to delivering superior value to our clients

Integrity

We adhere to the highest ethical standards and pledge to be transparent, just and consistent in word and deed. High level of accountability and reliability.
